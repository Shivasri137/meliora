Activities

Our summer camp activities are carefully curated to provide a holistic creative education. Each workshop is led by experienced educators and professional artists who are passionate about inspiring the next generation of creative thinkers.

Campers get the opportunity to try every discipline, discover their strengths, and collaborate with peers from diverse backgrounds. By the end of camp, every child leaves with tangible projects they've created — and memories that last a lifetime.

The program is structured to balance guided instruction with free creative exploration, ensuring that every child has the space to develop their own unique voice and artistic identity.

Film Making

In our Film Making workshop, young storytellers learn the art of visual storytelling from script to screen. Campers work in teams to write, direct, film, and edit their own short films using professional-grade equipment.

They develop collaboration, communication, and technical skills while unleashing their imagination. From drama to documentary, comedy to animation concepts, the possibilities are endless.

At the end of the camp, we host a special screening night where families and friends are invited to watch the completed films — a proud moment for every young filmmaker.

Cooking

The Cooking workshop celebrates food as a form of creative expression and cultural connection. Under the guidance of our culinary instructors, campers explore global cuisines, baking techniques, and healthy cooking practices.

From kneading dough to decorating cookies, children develop fine motor skills, mathematical thinking, and an appreciation for different cultures through the universal language of food.

Each session culminates in a delicious creation that campers get to enjoy together — turning cooking into a shared, joyful experience that builds community and confidence.

Crafting

Our Crafting workshop turns everyday materials into extraordinary creations. Campers explore a wide variety of craft traditions including origami, textile arts, clay modeling, and upcycled art projects.

Through crafting, children develop patience, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills. They learn that creativity isn't just about talent — it's about persistence, experimentation, and finding beauty in the process.

Each camper takes home a collection of handmade treasures that showcase their growth as an artist and maker throughout the camp experience.

Poetry

The Poetry workshop opens doors to the world of language, rhythm, and emotion. In a supportive and encouraging environment, young writers discover the power of words to express their innermost thoughts and feelings.

From haiku to spoken word, limerick to free verse, campers explore different poetic forms and find their own distinctive voice. Regular sharing circles build confidence in public speaking and listening skills.

Our camp publication features poems written by each camper — a keepsake anthology that celebrates the collective creative voice of every young poet at Meliora.

Painting

The Painting workshop is a vibrant exploration of color, texture, and visual expression. Campers work with watercolors, acrylics, and mixed media across a variety of surfaces — from traditional canvas to rocks, fabric, and found objects.

Our experienced art instructors introduce campers to different painting traditions and techniques, from impressionist brushwork to abstract expressionism, allowing each child to find their own artistic style.

The camp's final art showcase displays every camper's painting masterpiece — a gallery walk that celebrates the diversity of creative vision among our young artists.

Pottery

The Pottery workshop is a grounding and meditative creative experience that connects children with one of humanity's oldest art forms. Campers learn to shape, mould, and sculpt clay on a wheel and by hand, turning raw earth into beautiful, functional art objects.

Working with clay develops focus, fine motor control, patience, and a deep sense of satisfaction from creating something tangible. Children experience the full pottery process — from wedging the clay, building the form, to decorating and glazing their pieces.

Each camper fires and takes home their own unique pottery creations — a lasting reminder of their skill, creativity, and the meditative joy of working with their hands.

Robotics

The Robotics workshop ignites curiosity and logical thinking through hands-on engineering and coding. Campers work in teams to design, build, and program robots using age-appropriate kits and platforms that make technology creative and accessible.

Through robotics, children develop problem-solving skills, computational thinking, and teamwork. They learn how to break complex challenges into manageable steps, test solutions, and iterate — core skills that apply to any field of endeavour.

The workshop culminates in a mini robotics challenge where teams showcase their creations in friendly competitions — a thrilling, confidence-building celebration of STEM creativity at its most fun.

Crocheting

The Crocheting workshop introduces campers to the timeless art of yarn craft, where loops and hooks transform simple thread into beautiful, three-dimensional creations. Beginners start with basic stitches and progress to creating their own wearable or decorative pieces.

Crocheting is a wonderfully tactile, rhythmic activity that promotes mindfulness, concentration, and mathematical thinking — campers learn pattern reading, counting, and spatial reasoning while creating colourful, tangible art.

From amigurumi animals to cosy accessories and decorative items, every camper completes real projects they can wear, gift, or display — building a skill that lasts long after camp ends.
